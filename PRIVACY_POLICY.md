# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 2026**

## 1. Introduction

Pursue Media ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website and platform.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

- **Account Information:** When you create a Pursue Media account, we collect your name, email address, password, and other account details.
- - **Profile Information:** Information about your business, industry, and preferences.
  - - **TikTok Account Information:** When you authorize Pursue Media to access your TikTok account, we collect and store certain information, including your TikTok user ID, username, and basic profile information.
    - - **Content:** Any content (videos, images, captions, hashtags) you upload or create through our platform.
      - - **Communication Data:** Messages, support tickets, and feedback you send us.
       
        - ### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
       
        - - **Device Information:** We collect information about the devices you use to access Pursue Media, including device type, operating system, and browser type.
          - - **Log Data:** Our servers automatically record information when you use our platform, including IP addresses, access times, pages viewed, and referring URLs.
            - - **Cookies and Tracking Technologies:** We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience and analyze platform usage.
              - - **Analytics:** We use analytics services to understand how users interact with our platform.
               
                - ### 2.3 Information from Third Parties
               
                - - **Social Media Platforms:** When you connect your TikTok account, we may receive additional information from TikTok as permitted by their API.
                 
                  - ## 3. How We Use Your Information
                 
                  - We use the collected information for:
                 
                  - - **Service Delivery:** To provide, maintain, and improve Pursue Media services.
                    - - **Content Management:** To manage and post content to your social media accounts.
                      - - **Account Management:** To manage your account and send you service-related announcements.
                        - - **Communication:** To respond to your inquiries and send you promotional materials (with your consent).
                          - - **Analytics:** To analyze usage patterns and improve our platform.
                            - - **Security:** To detect, prevent, and address fraud and technical issues.
                              - - **Compliance:** To comply with legal obligations.
                               
                                - ## 4. How We Share Your Information
                               
                                - ### 4.1 Third-Party Service Providers
                               
                                - We may share information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or servicing you. These service providers are contractually obligated to use your information only as necessary to provide services to us.
                               
                                - ### 4.2 Social Media Platforms
                               
                                - When you authorize us to post content to TikTok, we must share your content with TikTok's servers. We only share information necessary for the service you request.
                               
                                - ### 4.3 Legal Obligations
                               
                                - We may disclose your information when required by law or when we believe in good faith that such disclosure is necessary to:
                               
                                - - Comply with legal obligations
                                  - - Protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
                                    - - Protect against fraud or security threats
                                     
                                      - ### 4.4 Business Transfers
                                     
                                      - If Pursue Media is involved in a merger, acquisition, bankruptcy, dissolution, reorganization, or similar transaction, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.
                                     
                                      - ## 5. Data Security
                                     
                                      - We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure.
                                     
                                      - ### 5.1 TikTok API Tokens
                                     
                                      - Your TikTok API tokens and credentials are:
                                      - - Encrypted and stored securely
                                        - - Never shared with third parties
                                          - - Only used to perform actions you authorize
                                            - - Revocable at any time through your account settings
                                             
                                              - ## 6. Data Retention
                                             
                                              - We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as long as necessary to provide you with our services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting support@pursuemedia.com.
